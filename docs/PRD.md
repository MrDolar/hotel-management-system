# Hotel Management - PRD

AI-powered hotel management system.

## Features
1. Room Management - CRUD, types, pricing, availability
2. Booking System - Create, check-in, check-out
3. Guest Management - Guest profiles
4. AI Smart Pricing - Dynamic price suggestions
5. AI Occupancy Prediction - Forecast occupancy rates
6. AI Customer Service - Chatbot for guests
